Polymarket & Kalshi Arbitrage Trading Bot

This project is a high-performance arbitrage bot designed for prediction markets such as Polymarket and Kalshi. It identifies pricing inefficiencies between markets and executes trades automatically.

Key Features

  • Real-time market monitoring
  • Automated arbitrage execution
  • Low-latency trade replication
  • Risk-aware capital allocation

Use Cases

Suitable for developers and traders building automated systems for prediction markets, arbitrage strategies, and quantitative trading.
